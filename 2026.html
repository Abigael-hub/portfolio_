I am a research, monitoring and evaluation (M&E), and impact evaluation consultant with over five years of experience supporting international NGOs, development partners, and private sector clients to generate evidence for decision-making.

My work spans social and market research, programme evaluation, and applied data analysis, including baseline and endline studies, quasi-experimental impact evaluations, A/B testing, and mixed-methods research across governance, financial inclusion, youth, and civic engagement programmes.

I combine strong statistical expertise (R, Stata, Python, SPSS, SQL) with practical M&E experience to deliver donor-ready reports, dashboards, and learning briefs that inform programme adaptation, accountability, and scale.
