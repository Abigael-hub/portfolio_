Profile

Abigael Cherotich is a Monitoring, Evaluation, and Research Consultant with over seven years of experience leading large‑scale surveys, longitudinal evaluations, and mixed‑methods studies across East Africa.

Her work spans education, adolescent health, SRHR, governance, gender, and economic empowerment programs. She has delivered donor‑facing research for international NGOs, foundations, and consulting firms.

Core strengths include quasi‑experimental design, sampling strategy, data quality systems, advanced analytics, and translating evidence into actionable policy insights.
