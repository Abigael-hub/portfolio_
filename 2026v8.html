Profile

Abigael Cherotich is a Monitoring, Evaluation, and Research Consultant with over seven years of experience leading large‑scale surveys, longitudinal evaluations, and mixed‑methods studies across East Africa.

Her work spans education, adolescent health, SRHR, governance, gender, and economic empowerment programs. She has delivered donor‑facing research for international NGOs, foundations, and consulting firms.

Core strengths include quasi‑experimental design, sampling strategy, data quality systems, advanced analytics, and translating evidence into actionable policy insights.

Monitoring, Evaluation & Research Services

Monitoring & Evaluation

Longitudinal research, mixed-methods baseline, midline, endline, and impact evaluations. Design of monitoring frameworks, KPIs, indicators, and results measurement plans. Process monitoring and quality assurance to ensure reliable program implementation.

Data Analysis & Visualization

End-to-end research from questionnaire and survey design, data collection, cleaning, and analysis. Advanced statistical analysis using R, Python, STATA, SPSS. Dashboard development, automated reporting, and actionable insights for donors and stakeholders.

Capacity Building

Training workshops, mentoring, and institutional support to strengthen M&E systems. Guidance on tools, methodologies, and best practices to enhance team and organizational performance. Knowledge-sharing sessions to enable continuous learning and adaptation.
