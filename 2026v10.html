About Me

I am a Monitoring, Evaluation, and Statistical Analysis Consultant with over 7 years of experience leading complex evaluations, large-scale surveys, and analytical projects across East Africa. My work integrates rigorous quantitative analysis with qualitative research to provide actionable, decision-ready evidence for governments, donors, and development partners.

I specialize in mixed-methods evaluations that not only measure program effectiveness but also explain how and why results are achieved. My expertise spans quasi-experimental and experimental designs, causal inference techniques, longitudinal studies, survey statistics, and process evaluations.

I have supported international NGOs, foundations, and consulting firms in sectors including education, adolescent health, SRHR, governance, gender equality, and economic empowerment. Across all assignments, I emphasize methodological rigor, actionable insights, and stakeholder engagement.

My approach bridges statistical precision and real-world context — ensuring evidence is not only credible but also usable for strategic decision-making and program improvement.

Leadership & Value

I lead end-to-end evaluation assignments — from scoping and design to data collection, analysis, and reporting. My work helps organizations make informed decisions, optimize programs, and maximize impact.

I provide the expertise, flexibility, and direct engagement of a principal consultant while delivering rigorous evidence comparable to what large consulting firms offer.

Key differentiators: senior-level quantitative expertise, multi-sector experience, mixed-methods capability, and a track record of delivering high-quality insights for international development programs.
