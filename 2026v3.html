I am a research, monitoring and evaluation (M&E), and impact evaluation consultant with over five years of experience supporting international NGOs, development partners, and private sector clients to generate evidence for decision-making.

My work spans social and market research, programme evaluation, and applied data analysis, including baseline and endline studies, quasi-experimental designs, and mixed-methods research across governance, financial inclusion, youth, and civic engagement programmes.

I combine strong statistical expertise with practical MEL experience to deliver donor-ready reports, dashboards, and learning products that inform policy, programme adaptation, and accountability.
